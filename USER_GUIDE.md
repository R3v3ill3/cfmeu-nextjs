## Quick Start Guide

### For New Field Organisers (5-Minute Setup)

**Step 1: Install the PWA on Your Phone**
- Open the app URL in Safari (iOS) or Chrome (Android)
- Look for "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App" option
- Tap to install - this creates an app icon on your home screen
- The app works offline and syncs when you're back online

**Step 2: Your First Day Dashboard**
- Open the app and sign in with your CFMEU credentials
- Your Dashboard shows your assigned patches with key metrics
- Each patch card shows projects, EBA coverage, and compliance status
- Tap any patch to see your geographic area

**Step 3: Find Your Closest Project**
- From the Dashboard, tap **"Start Mapping"** or **"Closest to me"**
- Enable location services when prompted
- The map shows projects sorted by distance from your current location
- Tap a project pin to see details and start your site visit

**Step 4: Complete Your First Site Visit**
- At the project site, use the **Site Visit Wizard** (floating button on project pages)
- Walk through the wizard steps:
  - **Mapping**: Add employers, delegates, and workers
  - **Compliance**: Check CBUS, Incolink, and EBA status
  - **Ratings**: Assess employer performance
- The wizard auto-saves your progress - works even offline

## Getting Started with Mobile

### Installing the Progressive Web App (PWA)

**On iPhone/iPad:**
1. Open Safari and navigate to the app URL
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Confirm by tapping **"Add"**
5. The CFMEU app icon appears on your home screen

**On Android:**
1. Open Chrome and navigate to the app URL
2. Tap the **Menu** button (three dots)
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"** or **"Install App"**
4. Tap **"Install"** to confirm
5. The app installs on your device like a native app

**PWA Benefits:**
- Works offline - perfect for construction sites with poor connectivity
- Faster loading than web browsers
- Send you push notifications for urgent updates
- Access to device features like camera and GPS
- Automatic background updates

### First Login and Setup

**Initial Sign In:**
1. Open the app from your home screen
2. Enter your CFMEU email and password
3. Grant permissions when prompted:
   - **Location Services**: For finding nearby projects
   - **Camera**: For taking photos during site visits

4. Complete your profile setup if prompted

**Understanding Your Dashboard:**

- **Metrics**: Real-time data on projects, members, and compliance
- **Quick Actions**: Fast access to mapping, compliance, and ratings
- **Alerts**: Urgent items needing your attention
- **Recent Activity**: Your latest site visits and assessments

### Navigation Basics

**Mobile Navigation:**
- **Bottom Navigation**: Main sections - Dashboard, Projects, Map, Ratings, Tasks
- **Hamburger Menu**: Additional options and settings
- **Back Button**: Consistent back navigation throughout the app
- **Search**: Available on most list pages for quick filtering

**Touch Gestures:**
- **Tap**: Select items, open menus, confirm actions

## Field Organiser Guide

### Daily Workflow Overview

**Morning Preparation (5 minutes):**
1. Check Dashboard for urgent alerts and assigned tasks
2. Review patch metrics and identify priority projects
3. Plan your route using **"Closest to me"** feature
4. Download offline maps for areas with poor connectivity
5. Charge your phone and portable battery pack

**During Site Visits:**
1. Use **Site Visit Wizard** for structured data collection
2. Take photos of safety concerns, non-compliance, or positive examples
3. Record voice notes for detailed observations
4. Rate employers using the Traffic Light Rating System
5. Create delegate tasks for follow-up actions
6. Use **Check In** feature to document your presence

**End of Day Wrap-up (10 minutes):**
1. Review completed site visits and assessments
2. Follow up on urgent issues identified during visits
3. Update any incomplete assessments
4. Plan next day's priorities
5. Sync all data (automatic, but verify completion)

**Before Arriving:**
- Research project status and employer history
- Review previous site visit notes
- Check for any outstanding compliance issues
- Prepare specific questions or areas to focus on

**On Site Process:**

**Step 1: Project Check-In**

- Note site conditions and safety observations
- Identify key contacts and decision-makers

**Step 2: Site Walkthrough**
- Document safety compliance and concerns
- Talk with workers about conditions and issues
- Identify union members and potential delegates
- Note any contractor or subcontractor changes

**Step 3: Employer Engagement**
- Meet with site management or supervisors
- Discuss any compliance concerns or issues
- Review CBUS and Incolink compliance
- Assess union relationship and cooperation

**Step 4: Data Collection**
- Use **Site Visit Wizard** to systematically collect data
- Take photos of relevant conditions or documentation
- Record detailed notes about observations
- Complete any required assessments

**Step 5: Delegate Coordination**
- Meet with union delegates on site
- Discuss issues and provide guidance
- Assign specific tasks for follow-up
- Schedule regular check-ins

**Step 6: Wrap-Up**
- Complete all assessments in the wizard
- Ensure data is saved and synced when possible
- Schedule follow-up actions or meetings
- Document any immediate outcomes or agreements

### System Overview

The **Traffic Light Rating System v2** is a comprehensive assessment tool that evaluates employer compliance and relationship quality using a simple, visual traffic light color scheme. The system uses dual-track assessment methodology combining quantitative project data with qualitative organiser expertise.

### Two-Track Assessment System

**Track 1: Project Data (Quantitative)**
- **Data Sources**: Compliance checks, site visits, delegate reports
- **Metrics**: CBUS compliance, Incolink payments, safety incidents, disputes
- **Weighting**: Typically 60-70% of final rating
- **Update Frequency**: Updated with each compliance assessment
- **Objectivity**: Based on verifiable data and documentation

**Track 2: Organiser Expertise (Qualitative)**
- **Data Sources**: Organiser knowledge and experience with employer
- **Metrics**: Relationship quality, industry reputation, cooperation level
- **Weighting**: Typically 30-40% of final rating
- **Update Frequency**: Updated with each organiser assessment
- **Subjectivity**: Based on professional judgment and experience

**Combined Rating Calculation:**
1. Individual assessment scores are collected from both tracks
2. Each assessment type applies specific weightings
3. Scores are combined using weighted averaging
4. Final numeric score converts to traffic light rating
5. Confidence levels indicate assessment reliability

### Assessment Categories and Criteria

**Union Respect Assessment (25% Weighting)**
1. **Right of Entry**: Ease of union access to workplace
2. **Delegate Accommodation**: Support for union delegates and their duties
3. **Access to Information**: Transparency and willingness to share relevant information
4. **Access to Inductions**: Union involvement in worker induction processes
5. **EBA Status**: Current Enterprise Bargaining Agreement coverage and compliance

**Safety Assessment (25% Weighting)**
1. **Site Safety**: Overall safety standards and workplace conditions
2. **Safety Procedures**: Implementation and adherence to safety protocols
3. **Incident Reporting**: Transparency in reporting and learning from incidents

**Subcontractor Assessment (20% Weighting)**
1. **Subcontractor Usage**: Fairness in subcontractor selection and treatment
2. **Payment Practices**: Timely payment to subcontractors and suppliers
3. **Sham Contracting**: Absence of illegal employee misclassification

**Role-Specific Assessment (30% Weighting)**
- **Head Contractors**: Project management, industry reputation, financial stability
- **Subcontractors**: Work quality, reliability, technical competence
- **Trade Contractors**: Expertise level, workmanship, professional standards
- **Labour Hire**: Worker treatment, compliance, industry practices

### Confidence Levels

**Assessment Confidence Indicators:**
- **High Confidence**: Direct, recent experience (within 90 days) with strong evidence
- **Medium Confidence**: Reliable information with adequate evidence (within 120 days)
- **Low Confidence**: Limited information with minimal evidence
- **Very Low Confidence**: Very limited information, primarily indirect knowledge

**Impact on Ratings:**
- Higher confidence assessments carry more weight in calculations
- Multiple high-confidence assessments create rating stability
- Low confidence ratings may require additional verification
- Confidence levels help identify areas needing more assessment

### Sham Contracting Detection

Sham contracting occurs when employers misclassify employees as independent contractors to avoid legal obligations like payroll tax, superannuation, workers' compensation, and leave entitlements.

**Detection and Impact:**
- **Hard Block**: Sham contracting flags prevent green ratings (maximum Yellow/Amber)
- **Required Evidence**: Detailed notes and documentation required for flags
- **Assessment Locations**: Can be flagged in compliance checks, subcontractor assessments, and expertise ratings
- **Audit Trail**: Complete history of flags, evidence, and resolutions maintained

**Flagging Process:**
1. Identify evidence of sham contracting during assessments
2. Toggle "Evidence of Sham Contracting Detected" switch
3. Provide detailed notes explaining the evidence and circumstances
4. Include specific examples, dates, and supporting information
5. Submit assessment with sham contracting flag

**Resolution Process:**
1. Employer addresses sham contracting issues
2. Evidence provided showing correction of practices
3. Union verification that issues are resolved
4. Clearing reason documented in audit trail
5. Rating cap removed for future assessments

### Common Mobile App Issues

**App Performance Problems:**

**Slow Loading or Freezing:**
- **Restart App**: Close and reopen the app to resolve temporary issues
- **Device Restart**: Restart your device to clear memory and system issues
- **Check Storage**: Ensure adequate storage space on your device
- **Update App**: Check for and install any available app updates
- **Clear Cache**: Clear app cache through browser settings if using web app

**Battery Drain Issues:**
- **Location Services**: Reduce GPS usage when not needed for site visits
- **Background Refresh**: Limit background app refresh for non-essential apps
- **Screen Brightness**: Lower screen brightness during extended use
- **Battery Saver**: Use device battery saver mode when appropriate
- **Charging Strategy**: Keep device charged during heavy usage periods

**Storage Space Issues:**
- **Photo Management**: Review and delete unnecessary photos and videos
- **Cache Clearing**: Clear app cache and old offline data
- **Data Cleanup**: Remove old completed tasks and historical data
- **Cloud Backup**: Move important data to cloud storage
- **Storage Upgrade**: Consider device storage upgrade if regularly running out of space

**Offline Sync Problems:**

**Sync Failures:**
- **Check Connection**: Verify internet connection is stable and working
- **Manual Sync**: Try manual sync by pulling down to refresh or using sync button
- **Queue Management**: Check sync queue for stuck items and retry individually
- **Restart Device**: Restart device to resolve temporary connection issues
- **Contact Support**: Contact support if sync issues persist

**Conflicting Data:**
- **Review Conflicts**: Review any data conflicts and choose correct version

- **Backup Check**: Check backup data to verify correct information
